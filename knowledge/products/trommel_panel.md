# Tega Trommel Panel — Modular

## Description

Modular screening panel for trommel drums, available in cast
polyurethane or molded rubber, with interchangeable hole patterns for
different separation duties. Mounted via pin-and-sleeve or strap fixing
onto standard trommel drum frames.

## Available specifications

| Parameter | Range / Options |
|---|---|
| Panel size | 305 x 305 mm, 610 x 305 mm |
| Thickness | 25 mm, 30 mm, 40 mm, 50 mm |
| Aperture shapes | Round, square, slotted |
| Aperture size | 6 mm – 80 mm (duty-dependent) |
| Open area | 35% – 55% depending on aperture/pitch |
| Material — PU | 85, 90, 95 Shore A, cast polyurethane |
| Material — rubber | 60–65 Shore A, natural rubber compound |
| Fixing profile | Pin-and-sleeve (top-fix), strap-fix, bolt-through |
| Min service thickness | 15 mm (per `rules/design_rules.md`) |

## Material guidance

| Ore/material condition | Recommended material |
|---|---|
| High-silica, dry, abrasive ore | Polyurethane (superior abrasion resistance) |
| Wet, high-impact, large lumps | Rubber (superior impact/cut resistance) |
| Sticky/clay-bearing ore | Polyurethane (lower friction, better self-cleaning) |
| Mixed/variable feed | Hybrid panel sets — evaluate per duty zone |

See `rules/design_rules.md` for full material selection rule and
tolerance limits, and `rules/standards.md` for QA/test standards.
